services

Our services are well tailored to suit the need of exporters, importers who seek to maximize the numerous benefits and at the same time minimize the risks associated with foreign trade within the Nigerian market space. We also represent international buyers in the Nigerian market by negotiating and sourcing for a wide range of commodities on their behalf.

haulage services

REGAL SEGADE LTD creates a strategic supply chain and logistics platform for the supply  and distribution of goods from one source to the last mile. We provide transport solutions to leading companies in Nigeria. export brokerage

Our years of experience in the export business has given us a platform to connect foreign buyers of agro-commodities and mineral resources with local suppliers. We also ensure that due diligence is carried out about the contracting parties in order to ensure smooth business relationship.

consultancy

We are suitably positioned because of our wealth of experience to offer expert solutions and consultancy services to new entrants and existing firms in the export business needed to make them successful. We are a company to talk to for export trading support, and guidance. Our team of seasoned professionals are always ready to provide you effective and efficient solutions.
