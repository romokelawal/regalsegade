about us

REGAL SEGADE LTD is one of the leading export trading company in Nigeria. We are a diversified and Integrated company registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and licensed by the Nigeria Export Promotion Council (NEPC) to carry out a number of business operations among which are exportation of agricultural commodities, mineral resources, export brokerage, consultancy and haulage.Our business is built on the ethos of giving our customers exactly what they want, where and when they need it.

Through our experience in the industry, we have been able to export top quality commodities to different countries in the world including Germany, Portugal, Israel, Turkey, United Kingdom (U.K), United Arab Emirates (U.A.E), Jordan etc.
